Hey! I'm Murto.

I'm passionate about entrepreneurship and using biology as a technology to solve real-world problems, so I'm always looking for opportunities to learn and grow in the world of compbio.   • developing a deep learning model to predict peptide-IDP binding sites

In my spare time, I enjoy writing, staying fit, Muay Thai, and reading about the latest in tech and biotech.
